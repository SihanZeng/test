About My Hobbies

Hello! My hobbies can be divided into three sections. Those are: Gaming, Sports, and Music. Below will contain more specific information about those hobbies.

Gaming

Currently, my favorite game is VALORANT, a competitive FPS (first-person shooter) game. A close second is Phasmophobia, a co-op indie psychological horror game. Other games that I do play fairly often are League of Legends and Genshin Impact.

VALORANT

VALORANT features many different gamemodes, but the main ones are the ones featured in Unrated and Competitive. There, you play on a team of five against another team of five where your goal is to reach 12 points before the enemy team.
My favorite agent is Reyna, although I do play Sova when she isn't available. My highest rank was Silver 3, though I don't usually play ranked to climb.

League of Legends

League of Legends' main gamemode is summoner's rift, where I play in the top lane with Mordekaiser or Tryndamere. I also play Teemo when I want my opponents to suffer, or Blitzcrank support in the bot lane.

Genshin Impact

In Genshin, I am currently Adventure Rank 50 with my main team consisting of Razor (Lvl. 90), Zhongli (Lvl. 80), Xingqiu (Lvl. 80), and Bennett (Lvl. 80). I don't focus on spiral abyss that much, but I did beat floors 1-8 and usually do floor 9.

Phasmophobia

I usually aim for maxouts, which is getting as much money as possible in a single mission. I've beated every map solo except Asylum which I haven't gotten to yet. If you wish to learn more about this game, please click here to see my guide on it.


Sports

My three favorite sports are Table Tennis, Badminton, and Swimming. Table Tennis and Badminton are non-contact racket sports where you play against an opponent(s), with swimming referring to moving in water from one point to another.

Table Tennis

I've played Table Tennis competitvely for three years, where I've been training with a coach and went to two competitions with one of the being on a team. It is my favorite sport due to its difficulty yet possible options that present themselves every second.

Badminton

Badminton is easily my second favorite sport. I enjoy the thinking yet physically demanding nature of the sport.

Swimming

I've currently doing Bronze Med in swimming (though it isn't available due to COVID). Just like Badminton, I enjoy the physically demanding nature and thinking as to what stroke to use in certian situations.
